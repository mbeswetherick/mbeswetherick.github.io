Harmony

Harmony is a simplified digital instrument. The only possible notes one can play are diatonic - meaning, you can't play anything that sounds "bad" or "wrong." You can use it to help you figure out chords/harmonies. I think it's interesting to make a chord and then toggle notes on and off to see how they affect the overall sound.
